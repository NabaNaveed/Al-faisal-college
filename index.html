ABOUT US

Al-Faisal College currently operates 3 schools located in Auburn, Campbelltown (Minto) and Liverpool (Austral). In 2022 Al-Faisal College - Lakemba will commence, which is a k-6 school. We cater for boys and girls across K-12 (Auburn, Campbelltown and Liverpool schools). Our school is open to all families in the community and enjoys a very positive reputation.

OUR VISION

Al-Faisal College students will aim to achieve Faith, Knowledge and Success through the provision of a wide range of learning experiences in a supportive and caring environment.
